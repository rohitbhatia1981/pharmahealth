Useful Links

Pharma Health is very keen on empowering all patients with knowledge of diseases and treatments associated with men’s health, as well other wider conditions. We want all our patients to live healthy lifestyles, which will inevitably impact positively on their overall health, thereby having fewer medical needs.

It is essential that all our patients are aware of the concept of healthy living and are able to learn about certain medical conditions from reliable and authentic websites. With this in mind, we are providing a list of websites that our patients can visit to enhance their knowledge of some of the common ailments that affect men’s health.

Disclaimer - The medical information and advice in the following websites has been provided by ProMan Health for informational purposes only. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice and should not be relied on as health or personal advice.We ask you to always seek the guidance of your doctor or other qualified health professional with any questions you may have regarding your health or a medical condition. Never disregard the advice of a medical professional, or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on any of the following websites.
