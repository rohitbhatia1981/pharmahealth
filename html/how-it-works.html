How it works?

Select Treatment

Select the treatment you require for your condition. For each condition we have listed the different medications available with an explanation of their differences. If you require further advice you can contact us via email and our ProMan Pharmacist Team will be in touch by either email or phone within 24 hours.

Once you have decided on your chosen medication, click the purchase button to add it to your cart. You can either choose to continue shopping or simply checkout and proceed with payment. Please note, payment will only leave your account if & when the prescription has been approved by our clinical team.

STEP

1

Complete Medical Questionnaire

We require the completion of the online medical consultation before a prescription can be approved. The ProMan Pharmacist Team will review your completed medical consultation and confirm the safety and effectiveness of the chosen treatment. Most of the medications we provide are prescription only medicines, and therefore we are required by law to confirm the medication is suitable for you before a prescription can be issued. The medical consultation will take 3-5 minutes to complete on average. All the patient data will be stored securely and not shared with any third parties without your prior consent. We strongly recommend that you give consent for us to inform your GP of any medication we issue so they can update your medical records. However, you also have the option of informing your GP directly.

In some cases our clinical team may require additional information as part of their review of your online medical consultation. In such cases, they will contact you directly either via email or phone.

Once our clinical team review your online medical consultation, they willapprove your prescription request if the treatment is clinically appropriate for you. The payment will be taken at this stage, followed by the issuance of an electronic private prescription to our partner pharmacy. If you are a new patient, we will have to verify your identity as per the UK regulatory requirement prior to prescribing your medication

STEP

2

Collect Medication from Local Pharmacy

Your can collect your medication from our London based GPhC registered pharmacy partner. The pharmacy team will post the medication via Royal Mail in discreet packaging as per your delivery instructions. You may choose to provide an address other than your home address.
